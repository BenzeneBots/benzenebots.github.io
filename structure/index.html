Technical

Technical Subgroups
  • Design

    This group is responsible for leading brainstorming sessions and 3D modeling, as well as prototyping mechanisms for the 3 robots we make during the year.

  • Fabrication

    This group makes the mechanisms and subsets for the competition robots, and supervises the team in the completion of the robots.

  • Electrical

    This group works to bring various systems to make our robots come to life. They're responsible for the wiring and control systems, as well as the integration of pneumatics.

  • Programming

    This group writes the code for all of our robots. They work to debug systems and to also create our control systems.

Safety

This group is responsible for ensuring everyone's safety at the workshop and the competitions. They also teach lessons on safety.

Business

Business Subgroups
  • Sponsors and Communication

    This group works to promote the team and STEM though social media and documentation. They also get sponsors, retain them, and maintain relationships with them.​

  • Awards and Outreach

    This group works to organize award submissions and outreach events to promote STEM in the community. ​

  • Finance

    This group maintains the team's finances.
